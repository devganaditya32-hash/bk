Our Products

Complete Range of
Pelleting Solutions

From feed industry to biomass — we manufacture machinery for every pelleting need with state-of-the-art technology.

🐄
Cattle Feed Mills

High-throughput cattle feed manufacturing plants with precision dosing, grinding, mixing, and pelleting for optimal feed quality.

🐔
Poultry Feed Mills

Complete poultry feed plants for broiler & layer nutrition, with conditioning and hygienization for feed safety compliance.

🐟
Aquatic Feed Mills

Specialized floating & sinking pellet manufacturing systems for fish, shrimp, and aquaculture feed processing.

🌾
Bran Pelleting Mills

Convert wheat, rice or other bran into dense, high-value pellets for animal feed and export markets.

🫘
Hull Pelleting Mills

Process soybean, sunflower and other crop hulls into uniform pellets for efficient storage and transport.

🌿
Grass Pelleting Mills

Green fodder and dried grass pelleting systems for efficient livestock nutrition delivery.

🌱
Alfalfa Pellet Mills

Premium alfalfa dehydration and pelleting lines for high-protein animal feed production and export.

🪵
Wood Pellet Mills

Industrial-grade wood pelleting plants for biomass energy production, meeting global fuel pellet standards (ENplus, etc.).

🌾
Straw Pelleting Mills

Agricultural straw-to-pellet conversion systems for renewable energy and livestock bedding applications.

⚗️
Chemical Pelleting

Specialized pelleting systems for chemical and industrial applications requiring precise formulation and consistency.

🌻
Organic Fertilizer

Complete organic fertilizer granulation and pelleting lines for agricultural and waste-to-resource applications.

🫛
Soybean Processing

Full soybean processing lines including cleaning, roasting, extraction, and pelletizing for feed and oil industries.
